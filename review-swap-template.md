## How this thread works

1. **Post your PR** using the format below, in a new comment.
2. **Pick someone else's PR** from this thread that doesn't have a reviewer yet, and leave a real review on it (on their actual PR, not here).
3. Come back and drop one line: `Reviewed → <link>`.
4. You owe one review for every PR you post. That's the only rule.

Full guidelines, including what counts as a real review: [`CONTRIBUTING.md`](../../blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md)

---

### Post your PR like this

```markdown
**PR:** <link to your pull request>
**Repo context:** one sentence — what does this project do
**What I want feedback on:** be specific
**Owe a review to:** _(leave blank until you've picked one)_
```
