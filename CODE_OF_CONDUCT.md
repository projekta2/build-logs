# Code of Conduct

## The short version

Be the reviewer you'd want to get. Be specific, be kind, assume good faith. Disagreement is welcome — contempt is not.

## The standard version

This project follows the spirit of the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/). In short:

**Expected:**
- Respectful, specific, actionable feedback — on code, never on people
- Acknowledging good work as readily as you point out problems
- Accepting that a reviewer's suggestion is a suggestion, not a mandate

**Not acceptable:**
- Personal attacks, condescension, or "well actually" gatekeeping
- Dismissing a contribution because of the author's experience level
- Harassment of any kind, in this repo or carried over from elsewhere

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported by emailing **hello@projekta2.com** or by flagging a maintainer directly in the relevant thread. Reports are handled case by case — first violations typically get a direct, private note; repeated or severe violations result in removal from the thread and loss of posting access to future Review Swap threads.

This is a small, maintainer-run project, not a foundation — enforcement is fast and informal, not a formal tribunal. The goal is simply to keep this a place worth coming back to.
